Q4.Write the relationship of U.M.L.

Relationship in U.M.L is used to represent the connection between structural behaviour and Grouping thing.

It is also called a link that describes how two or more things can relate during the execution of a system.

Types of Relationship are:-
1.Association
2.Dependency
3.Generalization
4.Realization

1.Association:- Association represents the static relationship shared among objects of classes.

By default association is bi-directional, for e.g:- A customer purchasing a product and a product sold always has a customer associated with it.

Association can also be unidirectional we can use an arrow to denote a unidirectional association.

2.Dependency:- Dependency represent the sematic relationship between two class such that a change in the attributes and operation of the class affects the attributes and operation of the other classes. 3.Generalization:- Generalization represent how super class are related to sub classes super class are called base class and sub classes in know as derived class.

Generalization is like bottom up approach in which two or more entities of lower level combine to form a higher level entity if they have some property or character.

4.Realization:- U.M.L Modling, a realization is relationship between two models element in one model is client realizes the behaviour that the other model elements supplier specifies...!
